IDIPS

The 1st Summer School on Document Image Processing provided both, an objective overview and an in-depth analysis of the state-of-the-art of the research field and its current open issues. The courses were delivered by world-renowned researchers in the field, covering both theoretical and practical aspects of real problems of Document Image Processing, as well as examples of successful applications. A 5-day workshop offered the opportunity to young researchers and PhD students to explore their knowledge in Image Processing and Document Image Processing from basic to advanced level.
